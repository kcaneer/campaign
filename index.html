What is a constable?

While the constable used to be a very important role for the functioning of the court system, today a constable has few responsibilities. Michael's goal is to convert this role to become an elected position who focuses solely on the culture of Lexington. Someone who devotes their time and resources to making Fayette County the best place not only to live but also to visit.
